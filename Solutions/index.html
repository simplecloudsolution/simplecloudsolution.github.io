Security Solutions

For years, the bernes Group has offered a wide variety of effective solutions relative to Security Consulting, Investigations and Litigation Support. Examples include:
  • Recovered $1.6 Million worth of stolen routers resulting in the arrest and convictions of two perpetrators.
  • Successfully made undercover purchases of stolen software resulting in the recovery of the software, the identification of the perpetrator and conviction of the perpetrator (former employee).
  • Identified counterfeiters of software, constructed link analysis and assisted law enforcement make their case.
  • Provided executive protection during annual stockholder’s meetings.
  • Rendered many problematic employment terminations to compliant separations.
  • Gathered videotape evidence of fraudulent worker’s compensation claims.
  • Provided unbiased* security assessments of office buildings, warehouses and executive’s residences.
  • Provided security during the movement of $25 Million worth of high technology product from a company in Texas to California.
  • Identified contract employee who perpetrated a fraud against his employer resulting in the termination of the contractor and reimbursement by the contract company.
  • An individual was selling counterfeit products on an internet auction site. Our investigation resulted in the confiscation of piratical items from the advertiser, his supplier and in the identification of the importer. Multiple Cease and Desist orders were filed by the victim company.

* Security companies, that are also vendors of particular security products, may be influenced by the product they sell.
