Ethical Mission

Our security consulting, investigations and litigation support are conducted discretely, promptly and lawfully. Our clients come to us when they lack the time, staffing, skills and abilities to reliably and lawfully provide solutions to their security needs in sensitive and difficult matters. The majority are lawyers, public companies and entities that must adhere to ethical methods.

The Bernes Group will not be influenced to arrive at the desired result. We do not guarantee the outcome of an investigation, assessment or litigation. We let the facts and the law dictate the results.
